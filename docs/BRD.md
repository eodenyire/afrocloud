## Requirement Summary
- Deliver a full cloud-console experience across core IaaS building blocks.
- Enable multi-user, organization-scoped tenancy and collaboration.
- Support clear operational governance (billing, audit, policy, access).
- Provide implementation and release artifacts suitable for enterprise review.
