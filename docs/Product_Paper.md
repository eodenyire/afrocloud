# Africa Connect Cloud — Product Paper

## Product Thesis
Africa Connect Cloud is a sovereign, multi-tenant cloud platform focused on control-plane usability, edge-aware deployment patterns, and enterprise governance for African and global operators.

## Product Pillars
- **Cloud Foundations**: compute, storage, database, networking primitives.
- **Enterprise Governance**: IAM, audit, org-level controls, policy boundaries.
- **Developer Velocity**: APIs, IaC, SDK/CLI pathways.
- **Observability & Reliability**: telemetry, operational workflows, release discipline.

## Competitive Positioning
Borrows UX and control-plane concepts from major cloud vendors while prioritizing:
- data residency and sovereignty,
- edge and constrained-network reality,
- fast onboarding for startups and enterprises.

## Monetization (Phased)
1. Free starter tenant with bounded quotas.
2. Pro workloads (reserved resources + observability retention).
3. Enterprise plans (advanced IAM, private networking, support SLAs).
